Symptoms of Chronic Diseases

Diabetes

Diabetes symptoms depends on how high your blood sugar is. Some people, especially if they have prediabetes,gestational diabetes or type 2 diabetes,may not have symptomes.In type 1diabetes,symptoms tend to come on quickly and to more serve

  • feeling more thirsty than usual.
  • urinating often.
  • losing weight without trying.
  • presence of ketones in the urine.ketons are abyproduct of the breakdown of muscle and fat that happens when there's not enough available insulin.
  • Having blurry vision.
  • Having slow-healing sores.
  • Geeeting a lot of infections,such as gum,skin and vaginal infections.

Blood Pressure

Most people with high blood pressure have no symptoms,even if blood pressure readings reach dangerously high levels. you can have high blood pressure for years without any symptoms.

A few people with high blood pressure may have:

  • Headaches
  • Shortness of breath
  • nosebleeds

Heart Problems

symptoms of coronary artery disease can include:

  • Chest pain,chest tightness,chest pressure and chest discomfort(angina).
  • Shortness of breath.
  • pain in the neck,jaw,throat,upper belly area or back pain ,numbness.
  • weakness or coldness in the legs or arms if the blood vessels in those body areas are narrowed.

Hypertension

Most people with hypertension don't feel any symptoms but usually some of the people can experience following symptoms:

  • Headaches
  • Nosebleeds
  • dizziness
  • chest pain
  • fatigue
